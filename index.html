Secrets Beneath the Deck - Rules

Objective: Uncover the hidden secrets beneath the ship’s deck while competing (or cooperating) with other players.

Game Setup

Player Roles:

You players are a group of investigators trying to figure out the murderer between the 6 suspects.

Gameplay

Actions Per Turn:

You as a group try to find the password and username to get the clues then with it figure out the murderer is. Winning Conditions: Cooperative Mode: All players win if the central mystery is solved. bonus thing: see how fast you could do it.
